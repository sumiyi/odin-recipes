Avocado Toast

Description

A quick yet tasty morning dish

Ingredients

Required

  • Your choice of bread
  • Avocado
  • Flaky Seasalt

Optional

  • Fried Egg

Steps

  1. Toast however many slices of bread
  2. Cut your avocado into thin strips
  3. Place the avocado on top of the toasted bread
  4. Add a little bit of flaky seasalt ontop of the avocado toast
  5. Add a fried egg (optional)

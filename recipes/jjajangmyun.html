Jjajangmyun

Description

Jjajangmyun is a delicious Korean dish that is savory, sweet, and slightly bitter. This dish contains plenty of vegetables with a chewy noodle to go with the sauce.

Ingredients

  • Jjajang Sauce
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Jjajang noodles
  • Potato starch
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Sautee the vegetables until slightly soft
  2. Mix the sauce with the vegetables and simmer for 10-15 minutes
  3. Add a bit of potato starch with water and add it to the sauce
  4. Stir the sauce vigoriously fully incorporating the starch slurry into the sauce
  5. Turn off the heat and place the lid on the pot to keep the sauce warm
  6. Boil water and add plenty of salt
  7. Add the noodles to the boiling water and follow package directions
  8. Drain the noodles and place in a serving dish
  9. Pour sauce we made ontop of the noodles and enjoy!
